About Us

Welcome to TruthLens Intelligence, an advanced AI-driven forensics platform engineered to combat the spread of misinformation and fake news across the digital landscape.

Our Mission

In an era where information spreads globally in seconds, distinguishing credible news from fabricated content is critical. Our mission is to restore digital trust by providing analysts, journalists, and everyday users with enterprise-grade fact-checking tools powered by sophisticated machine learning.

The Team

Our project was developed cooperatively as part of an academic endeavor for Review 2, bringing together unique expertise across distinct disciplines:

Backend & ML Integration

Managing the Calibrated Classifier Model, API endpoints, Flask architecture, and XAI feature analysis.

Frontend & UI/UX

Developing the responsive SaaS dashboard, custom CSS interactions, animations, and dark/light modes.

Data Pipeline & Extractors

Handling URL parsing, BeautifulSoup scraping protocols, ensuring clean HTML-to-text sanitization.

Testing & Oversight

Building the Admin Console, managing MongoDB deployments, dispute tracking, and system telemetry.
