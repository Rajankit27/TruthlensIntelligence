How TruthLens Works

TruthLens Intelligence utilizes a complex, multi-layered pipeline to analyze raw data and determine its journalistic integrity.

Step 1 Data Ingestion & Extraction

Users can submit either direct text or a news URL. If a URL is provided, our custom Python scrapers bypass boilerplate clutter, ads, and navigation to extract the core HTML text automatically.

Step 2 NLP Vectorization

The extracted text is forced through an aggressive pre-processing tokenizer—removing stop words, isolating punctuation, and reducing words to their root stems. It is then encoded by our TF-IDF Vectorizer into mathematical arrays.

Step 3 Machine Learning Inference

The matrix is passed into a Logistic Regression Model wrapped within a Calibrated Classifier. The system analyzes underlying semantic structures, emotional manipulation, and linguistic nuances indicative of fabrication, rather than just simple keywords.

Step 4 Domain Reputation Heuristics

To avoid severe false positives against trusted sources, the text and URL are cross-referenced against an internal dynamic whitelist of certified global news networks (BBC, Reuters, Times of India). Verified domains receive a strict probability offset.

Step 5 Explainable AI (XAI) Output

Finally, we calculate the top contributing feature coefficients to show you explicitly which words influenced the model to lean "FAKE" or "REAL", ensuring total insight transparency in the UI.
