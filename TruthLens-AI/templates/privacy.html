Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

TruthLens Intelligence collects user identity metadata to securely operate our intelligence logging platform. This includes email addresses, access tokens, and interaction behavior during URL or direct text analysis. We do not store payment or personal biometrics.

2. How We Use Data

Your inputs, including suspicious URLs and text queries, are strictly used for machine learning inference and heuristic analysis to verify news authenticity. Anonymized meta-features may be aggregated to improve the core detection engine's accuracy threshold over time.

3. Authentication & Security

We deploy standard JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for secure role-based access control. All communication to backend inference servers happens over encrypted channels. Intelligence Logs are strictly compartmentalized and inaccessible by unauthorized peers.

4. Data Retention

Unless expressly requested via our Data Portal, user intelligence histories are retained indefinitely to provide a seamless audit log capability to you. Administrator actions are logged in immutable audit trails.

If you have any questions, please contact our support team at privacy@truthlens.com.
